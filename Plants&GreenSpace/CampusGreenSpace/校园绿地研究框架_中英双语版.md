# 校园绿地研究框架 | Campus Green Space Research Framework

## 1. 项目定位 | Project Positioning

本项目基于中国校园绿地生态数据库，整合了地点、植物、鸟类和来源信息，可用于分析校园尺度的生物多样性结构、区域差异、环境梯度响应以及数据覆盖偏倚。

This project is based on a Chinese campus green-space ecological database that integrates locality, plant, bird, and source information. It can be used to analyze biodiversity structure, regional variation, environmental gradients, and data coverage bias at the campus scale.

本研究可参考 City Biodiversity Index, CBI（新加坡城市生物多样性指数）的结构逻辑，但不应直接照搬城市级评分体系。更合适的做法是建立一个校园尺度的 benchmark framework。

This research can refer to the structural logic of the City Biodiversity Index (CBI, Singapore Index on Cities' Biodiversity), but it should not directly copy the city-level scoring system. A campus-scale benchmark framework is more appropriate.

## 3. 研究价值 | Research Value

### 3.1 校园作为可比较生态单元 | Campuses as Comparable Ecological Units

校园绿地是城市内部一种相对稳定、边界较清晰、管理方式较明确的绿地类型，适合作为跨区域比较的生态单元。

Campus green spaces are a relatively stable, bounded, and managed type of urban green space, making them suitable comparative ecological units across regions.

### 3.2 生物名录与环境梯度结合 | Biodiversity Records Linked to Environmental Gradients

该数据集不仅有物种记录，还有面积、海拔、温度、降水、建校时间等变量，因此能够分析多样性与环境背景之间的关系，而不仅仅是列出物种清单。

The dataset includes not only species records but also area, elevation, temperature, precipitation, and establishment year, allowing biodiversity to be analyzed in relation to environmental context rather than only listing species.

### 3.3 植物乡土性与绿化结构研究 | Plant Nativeness and Greening Structure

植物数据包含乡土/非乡土和生长型信息，能够支持对校园绿化策略、植物配置结构和本土生态适应性的讨论。

The plant dataset includes nativeness and growth-form information, supporting discussions of campus greening strategies, planting structure, and local ecological adaptation.

### 3.4 鸟类迁徙功能研究 | Bird Migration-Related Ecological Function

鸟类数据包含迁徙状态，可用于讨论校园是否在城市生态网络中承担候鸟停歇地或留鸟栖息地的功能。

The bird dataset includes migratory status, making it possible to discuss whether campuses function as stopover sites for migratory birds or habitats for resident birds in urban ecological networks.

### 3.5 数据覆盖偏倚本身是研究对象 | Data Coverage Bias Is Itself a Research Topic

来源表使数据具有可追溯性，因此不仅可以研究生物多样性格局，还可以研究不同区域、不同校园之间的调查强度和知识生产偏差。

The source table makes the data traceable, so the project can study not only biodiversity patterns but also variation in survey effort and knowledge production across regions and campuses.

## 4. 具体研究问题 | Specific Research Questions

### 4.1 核心研究问题 | Core Research Question

校园绿地的生态系统的主要构成元素和他们之间的关系 （eg. 鸟类、植物、地理位置三者关系）  
The main constituent elements of the campus green space ecosystem and the relationships between them. (The interrelationship among birds, plants and geographical locations, etc.)

### 4.2 具体问题 | Specific Questions

1. 中国不同区域校园绿地的植物与鸟类多样性结构有何差异？
2. How does the biodiversity structure of plants and birds vary across campus green spaces in different regions of China?
3. 校园面积、海拔、温度、降水和建校年代是否与植物或鸟类丰富度相关？
4. Are campus area, elevation, temperature, precipitation, and establishment year associated with plant or bird richness?
5. 校园植物中的乡土与非乡土物种比例是否存在明显区域差异？
6. Does the proportion of native and nonnative plant species vary significantly across regions?
7. 校园植物生长型结构是否随气候条件和地理区位发生变化？
8. Does plant growth-form structure change with climate conditions and geographic location?
9. 鸟类迁徙型与留鸟型的组成是否在不同区域或不同校园类型中表现出不同格局？
10. Do migratory and resident bird assemblages show different patterns across regions or campus types?
11. 同时具有鸟类和植物记录的校园中，植物结构是否与鸟类组成存在潜在关联？
12. In campuses that contain both bird and plant records, is plant structure potentially associated with bird assemblage composition?
13. 目前观察到的生物多样性格局在多大程度上受到文献来源与调查覆盖不均的影响？
14. To what extent are the observed biodiversity patterns influenced by uneven literature sources and survey coverage?

- multi-taxon, abundance, and functional data

## 9. 推荐 Benchmark Workflow | Recommended Benchmark Workflow

1. 明确校园作为 benchmark 单元，并定义边界与比较口径。
  Define the campus as the benchmark unit and clarify its boundary and comparison logic.
2. 建立 campus profile，包括面积、气候、海拔、区域、建校年代等背景变量。
  Build a campus profile including area, climate, elevation, region, and establishment year.
3. 统一鸟类与植物名录的分类学和地点关联。
  Standardize taxonomy and locality linkage for bird and plant records.
4. 构建 biodiversity benchmark，包括丰富度、组成、乡土性和迁徙结构。
  Construct a biodiversity benchmark including richness, composition, nativeness, and migration structure.
5. 在区域、气候和面积梯度下进行比较。
  Compare patterns across region, climate, and area gradients.
6. 增加 data completeness benchmark，识别来源偏倚和覆盖缺口。
  Add a data completeness benchmark to identify source bias and coverage gaps.
7. 将现有结果映射到 CBI 的三大类，并标出可做、部分可做和无法评估的部分。
  Map current results to the three CBI dimensions and mark what is feasible, partially feasible, or not assessable.
8. 输出校园版 benchmark framework，而不是一个简单总分。
  Output a campus-scale benchmark framework rather than a single total score.
