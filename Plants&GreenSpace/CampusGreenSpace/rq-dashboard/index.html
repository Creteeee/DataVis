Main Benchmark Question
  1. Can plant diversity on campus represent the overall plant diversity of Shanghai?
  2. Is the proportion of alien plants in campus flora higher than that in Shanghai as a whole?
Why This Dataset Matters

These two datasets are important because they represent different ecological scales — Updated checklist of Shanghai vascular plants (2022) + Biodiversity dataset of vascular plants and birds in Chinese urban greenspace, city-wide plant diversity and human-curated campus ecosystems — allowing us to investigate how urban ecological structures are filtered and reshaped by human intervention.
